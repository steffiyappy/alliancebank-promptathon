🏢 Who is Aurora Nexus Global?

Aurora Nexus Global (ANG) is a mid-sized integrated financial services group offering retail banking, SME lending, corporate finance, and Islamic banking solutions. With over 3.2 million customers and 180 branches across the region, ANG has built its reputation on personalised service and deep community relationships.

Headquartered in the capital, ANG competes in one of the region's most dynamic financial markets, facing pressure from larger incumbents as well as digital-first challengers disrupting traditional banking models. The Group is at a pivotal inflection point: customers expect digital-first experiences, regulators demand tighter compliance, and internal operations remain heavily manual.

ANG is now executing its most ambitious transformation programme to date: Project Nexus 2027, a 3-year initiative to modernise operations, deepen customer relationships, and build a workforce ready for the AI era.

⚠️ The Burning Challenges Facing Aurora Nexus Global Today

  • Retail banking advisors spend 70% of their time on repetitive product queries instead of advising clients, and customer satisfaction is slipping
  • SME loan approval cycles average 14 days; customers expect a 48-hour decision and are walking to competitors
  • Training completion sits at just 34% group-wide, even as Project Nexus 2027 demands new skills across all functions
  • Monthly financial reporting requires 5 days of manual Excel consolidation before any analysis can begin
  • Over 60 regulatory updates were issued in the past year, and compliance teams are overwhelmed and operating reactively
  • Digital platform releases are delayed 2–3 weeks on average due to poor documentation and manual testing processes
  • Five different teams produce customer-facing content with no shared guidelines, and brand messaging is inconsistent
  • Project Nexus 2027 spans 47 initiatives, but 60% have no clearly accountable owner in leadership reviews
