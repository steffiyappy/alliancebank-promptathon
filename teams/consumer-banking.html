"The Invisible Advisor Problem"
← Back to All Teams

🏦Department Context

Alliance Bank's Consumer Banking division serves millions of retail customers across housing loans, personal loans, credit cards, fixed deposits, and wealth advisory. With over 100 branches and a growing digital channel (allianceonline), the division is at the forefront of Alliance Bank's Acceler8 growth strategy.

⚠️The Business Pain Point

Alliance Bank's retail banking advisors are spending 70% of their time answering the same repetitive product queries — home loan eligibility, credit card interest rates, fixed deposit promotions — instead of focusing on high-value financial advisory conversations. Customer satisfaction scores have dropped 8 points over the past year, and millennial customers are abandoning the branch experience for digital-native alternatives. Meanwhile, advisors feel underutilised and frustrated. The problem isn't the people — it's the absence of smart tools that can handle the routine so advisors can focus on relationships.

🎯Your Challenge

Complete all three tasks using Microsoft 365 Copilot. Document your prompts and outputs in your team's working file.

  1. Task 1: Use Copilot Chat to analyse 10 common retail banking queries (provided below) and identify the top 5 recurring themes. Suggest how each could be handled by an intelligent self-service tool. Common queries: "What is the current home loan interest rate?", "How do I increase my credit card limit?", "What documents do I need for a personal loan?", "How long does a loan approval take?", "What is the minimum balance for my savings account?", "Can I get a loan if I'm self-employed?", "How do I dispute a transaction on my card?", "What is the maximum tenure for a home loan?", "How do I apply for a fixed deposit?", "What happens if I miss a loan repayment?"

  2. Task 2: Use Copilot in Word to draft a brief for a 'Smart Advisor Assistant' — an AI-powered tool that answers the top repetitive queries, escalates complex cases to human advisors, and captures trending question gaps for the product team.

  3. Task 3: Use Copilot in PowerPoint to create a 3-slide executive summary showing the problem, your proposed solution, and the expected impact on customer satisfaction and advisor productivity.

🛠️Recommended Copilot Tools

Copilot Chat — query analysis & pattern recognition
Copilot in Word — brief drafting
Copilot in PowerPoint — executive narrative
Copilot in Outlook — draft advisor communication

🎲 Game Master Guide

This scenario works best with Copilot Chat (identifying recurring query themes), Copilot in Word (drafting the advisor brief), and Copilot Agent Builder (showing what a "Smart Advisor" agent could look like). Participants should focus on the advisor experience, not just the customer — the goal is to free up human time for high-value relationships. The Power Up that works best here is "Frontline Friendly" and "User Journey View".
