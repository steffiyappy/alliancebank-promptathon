🏦Department Context

Alliance Bank operates in one of Asia's most tightly regulated banking environments. The Risk & Compliance division is responsible for ensuring the Group meets all requirements from Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM), the Securities Commission, Bursa Malaysia, and international standards including Basel III and FATF AML requirements. The team manages credit risk, market risk, operational risk, and regulatory compliance across the Group.

⚠️The Business Pain Point

In 2024, Bank Negara Malaysia issued 47 new regulatory updates, circulars, and guidelines — an increase of 35% from the previous year. Alliance Bank's compliance team of 12 professionals is overwhelmed. Policies take an average of 3 months to update after a regulatory change. Staff in the branches and business units cannot easily find the latest version of a policy (SharePoint has 847 policy documents with inconsistent naming). Audit evidence is collected via email threads with no structured tracking, and exceptions are managed in ad hoc spreadsheets. During the last BNM audit, 3 findings were classified as "repeat" — meaning they had been raised before but not fully resolved.

🎯Your Challenge

Complete all three tasks using Microsoft 365 Copilot. Document your prompts and outputs in your team's working file.

  1. Task 1: Use Copilot Chat to analyse the following regulatory update summary and produce: (a) a plain-language summary of the key changes, (b) which Alliance Bank departments are affected, and (c) a 5-item compliance action checklist. Scenario: "BNM has issued a new guideline on Climate-related Financial Risk Management. Banks are required to conduct climate risk assessments for all large corporate lending, disclose climate risk exposure in annual reports, and embed climate risk into credit approval criteria by Q4 2025."

  2. Task 2: Use Copilot in Word to draft a Policy Update Notice for the new BNM climate risk guideline — covering: what's changing, who is affected, key deadlines, what staff must do, and where to find the full policy. Format it for distribution to all department heads.

  3. Task 3: Use Copilot in PowerPoint to create a 3-slide Compliance Dashboard overview for the Group Risk Committee: current regulatory exposure, key upcoming deadlines, and top 3 risk areas requiring management attention.

🎲 Game Master Guide

This scenario works best with Copilot in Word (policy clarity and drafting), Copilot Chat (gap analysis across regulations), Copilot in Excel (exception and evidence tracking), and Copilot Agent Builder (a "compliance assistant" that surfaces the latest policy, logs exceptions, and guides evidence collection). Teams should focus on execution visibility — who owns which compliance action and when. The Power Ups "Policy Draft", "Check for Blind Spots", and "Escalation Brief" are strongest here.
