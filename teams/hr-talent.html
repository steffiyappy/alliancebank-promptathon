🏦Department Context

Alliance Bank's HR & Talent division is responsible for the professional development and engagement of over 3,000 employees across the Group. Under the Acceler8 2027 transformation, HR plays a critical role in upskilling the workforce, embedding new ways of working, and driving a culture of continuous learning. The team publishes training, policies, and compliance requirements that all staff must engage with.

⚠️The Business Pain Point

Alliance Bank's HR team publishes over 40 policy documents and training modules each year — covering everything from code of conduct to anti-money laundering procedures to performance management changes. Despite mandatory status, training completion rates sit at just 34%. HR Business Partners (HRBPs) receive the same compliance questions repeatedly: "Is this policy applicable to me?", "Do I need to redo my AML training?", "Where do I find the updated leave policy?" The team spends more time answering individual queries than improving the training itself. And when regulatory updates require policy changes, it can take 3 months before updated content reaches all staff.

🎯Your Challenge

Complete all three tasks using Microsoft 365 Copilot. Document your prompts and outputs in your team's working file.

  1. Task 1: Use Copilot Chat to analyse the following list of 10 recurring HR queries and cluster them into themes. Then suggest how a self-service HR knowledge tool could address each cluster. Queries: "Do I need to complete the new AML training?", "What is the policy for medical leave?", "How do I apply for study leave?", "When is the performance review cycle?", "What happens if I miss the compliance deadline?", "Can I carry over unused annual leave?", "How do I raise a grievance?", "What is the policy on flexible working?", "Does this new BNM policy affect my team?", "How do I access my payslip?"

  2. Task 2: Use Copilot in Word to draft a 1-page "Policy Fast-Track" template — a structured format HR teams can use to communicate any new policy update in a clear, scannable way that employees will actually read. Include: what changed, who it affects, what you need to do, and deadline.

  3. Task 3: Use Copilot in PowerPoint to create a 3-slide proposal to the CHRO showing: the problem with current training engagement, your proposed "Ask HR" intelligent assistant concept, and the expected impact on HR team capacity and employee experience.

🎲 Game Master Guide

This scenario works best with Copilot Chat (pattern analysis of repeated questions), Copilot in Excel (correlating training completion with incident data), and Copilot Agent Builder (designing a role-based learning or "ask HR" agent). Teams should focus on an agent that answers, escalates, and learns from gaps — not just a static FAQ. The Power Ups "Convert to SOP", "Training Outline", and "Frontline Friendly" work particularly well here.
